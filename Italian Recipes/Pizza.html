Homemade Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

For the pizza dough:

- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup warm water
- 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp olive oil

For the pizza sauce:

- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1/2 tsp dried basil
- Salt and pepper to taste

For the pizza toppings:

- 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
- Fresh basil leaves
- Extra-virgin olive oil for drizzling

Instructions:

Prepare the pizza dough:

1. In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Add the frothy yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour mixture.
3. Stir until the dough comes together, then transfer it to a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until you have a smooth and elastic dough.
4. Form the dough into a ball and place it in a lightly oiled bowl. Cover it with a damp cloth and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 to 1.5 hours or until it doubles in size.

Prepare the pizza sauce:

1. In a saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for a minute or until fragrant.
2. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
3. Let the sauce simmer on low heat for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Adjust the seasoning to your taste.

Assemble and bake the pizza:

1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) or as high as your oven can go. If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
2. Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two equal portions. Roll out each portion on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a parchment-lined baking sheet or a pizza peel if you're using a pizza stone.
4. Spread a generous amount of the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
5. Add slices of fresh mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
6. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown, and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
7. Once out of the oven, top with fresh basil leaves and drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil.
8. Slice and serve hot! Enjoy your homemade Margherita pizza!
Feel free to get creative with the toppings and adjust the recipe according to your preferences. Happy pizza-making!
